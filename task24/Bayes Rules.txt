Bayes Rules

*It allows us to update our belief or knowledge about an event based on new evidence or information.
-given p(A) & p(*A)  --> prior probability
-p(A,B) = p(A)*p(B|A) --> joiat
-p(B) = p(A,B)+p(*A|B) --> normalier
-p(A|B) = p(A,B) / P(B) --> Posterior

It's equation
 P(A|B) = (P(B|A) * P(A)) / P(B)
 p(A,B) = P(B|A) * P(A)
 p(B) = p(A,B)+p(*A,B)
